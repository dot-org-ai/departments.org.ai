# Finance

> Business-as-Code definition for the Finance department category.

## Overview

Financial planning, accounting, treasury, and fiscal management

## Departments

| Department | Code | Description |
|-----------|------|-------------|
| Accounting | ACCT | General ledger, financial reporting, month-end close, and audit coordination |
| Financial Planning & Analysis | FPA | Budgeting, forecasting, variance analysis, and financial modeling |
| Treasury | TREAS | Cash management, banking relationships, investments, and liquidity management |
| Tax | TAX | Tax planning, compliance, filing, and transfer pricing |
| Accounts Payable | AP | Invoice processing, vendor payments, and expense management |
| Accounts Receivable | AR | Billing, collections, credit management, and cash application |
| Payroll | PAY | Payroll processing, tax withholding, and compensation disbursement |
| Internal Audit | AUDIT | Financial controls, process audits, and compliance verification |
