# Transport Operations

> Business-as-Code definition for the Transport Operations department category.

## Overview

Transportation and logistics functions including flight operations, fleet management, and dispatch

## Departments

| Department | Code | Description |
|-----------|------|-------------|
| Flight Operations | FOPS | Pilot scheduling, flight planning, aircraft dispatch, and operational control |
| Crew Scheduling | CREW | Crew rostering, duty time tracking, fatigue risk management, and reserve allocation |
| Ground Operations | GOPS | Gate management, ramp services, baggage handling, and aircraft turnaround coordination |
| Dispatch | DISP | Load assignment, route optimization, driver/vehicle dispatching, and real-time tracking |
| Fleet Management | FLEET | Vehicle acquisition, maintenance scheduling, fuel management, and fleet lifecycle optimization |
| Terminal Operations | TERM | Terminal management, passenger processing, cargo handling, and facility coordination |
| DOT Safety Compliance | DOTS | Hours-of-service compliance, vehicle inspection programs, driver qualification, and DOT audit readiness |
