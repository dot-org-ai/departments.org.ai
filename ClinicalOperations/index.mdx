# Clinical Operations

> Business-as-Code definition for the Clinical Operations department category.

## Overview

Healthcare delivery functions including nursing, pharmacy, medical records, and patient safety

## Departments

| Department | Code | Description |
|-----------|------|-------------|
| Nursing | NURS | Patient care delivery, nursing staff management, clinical protocols, and care coordination |
| Pharmacy | PHARM | Medication dispensing, formulary management, drug interaction review, and pharmacy compliance |
| Medical Records | HIM | Health information management, medical coding, chart completion, and records retention |
| Patient Safety | PSAFE | Patient safety programs, incident reporting, root cause analysis, and quality improvement |
| Radiology | RAD | Diagnostic imaging services including X-ray, CT, MRI, ultrasound, and interventional radiology |
| Laboratory | LAB | Clinical and anatomical pathology, specimen processing, and lab result reporting |
| Emergency Department | ED | Emergency triage, acute care, trauma response, and emergency medical services coordination |
| Admissions | ADMIT | Patient registration, bed assignment, insurance verification, and admission processing |
| Case Management | CMGMT | Care coordination, utilization review, discharge planning, and length-of-stay management |
| Infection Control | IC | Infection surveillance, outbreak investigation, hand hygiene compliance, and antimicrobial stewardship |
| Credentialing | CRED | Provider credentialing, privileging, peer review, and ongoing professional practice evaluation |
| Revenue Cycle Management | RCM | Charge capture, medical billing, claims submission, denial management, and payer contract optimization |
