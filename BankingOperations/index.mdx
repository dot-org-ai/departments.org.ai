# Banking Operations

> Business-as-Code definition for the Banking Operations department category.

## Overview

Specialized banking functions including lending, trading, trust, and compliance operations

## Departments

| Department | Code | Description |
|-----------|------|-------------|
| Underwriting | UW | Risk assessment, credit analysis, and approval decisions for loans, insurance, and securities |
| Loan Servicing | LOAN | Post-origination loan administration including payment processing, escrow, and modifications |
| Credit Risk | CRISK | Credit portfolio analysis, risk modeling, loss reserves, and credit policy governance |
| Trading Desk | TRADE | Securities and derivatives trading execution, market making, and position management |
| Trust Services | TRUST | Fiduciary management, estate administration, and trust account operations |
| Mortgage Operations | MORT | Mortgage origination, processing, closing, and secondary market activities |
| BSA/AML Compliance | AML | Bank Secrecy Act monitoring, anti-money laundering investigations, and suspicious activity reporting |
| Asset Management | AMGMT | Investment portfolio construction, fund management, and asset allocation strategy |
| Wealth Management | WMGMT | High-net-worth client advisory, financial planning, and investment management |
