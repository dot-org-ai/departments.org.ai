# Plant Operations

> Business-as-Code definition for the Plant Operations department category.

## Overview

Manufacturing and production functions including quality, maintenance, and industrial engineering

## Departments

| Department | Code | Description |
|-----------|------|-------------|
| Production Control | PROD | Production planning, work order management, capacity allocation, and output tracking |
| Quality Control | QC | Inspection, testing, statistical process control, and nonconformance management |
| Tooling and Die | TOOL | Tool design, die fabrication, jig and fixture management, and tooling maintenance |
| Maintenance Engineering | MENG | Preventive and predictive maintenance, equipment reliability, and maintenance scheduling |
| Process Engineering | PENG | Manufacturing process design, optimization, scale-up, and continuous improvement |
| Industrial Engineering | IENG | Work measurement, facility layout, ergonomics, and production efficiency analysis |
| Environmental Health & Safety | EHS | Workplace safety, environmental compliance, hazard identification, and OSHA reporting |
| Materials Management | MATL | Raw material procurement, inventory control, warehousing, and material requirement planning |
| Production Scheduling | SCHED | Master production scheduling, shop floor sequencing, and schedule adherence tracking |
| Receiving and Shipping | RSHIP | Inbound receiving, inspection, outbound shipping, freight coordination, and dock management |
| Laboratory Testing | LTEST | Quality assurance testing, chemical analysis, microbiological testing, and lab instrument calibration |
