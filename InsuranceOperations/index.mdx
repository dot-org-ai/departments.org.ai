# Insurance Operations

> Business-as-Code definition for the Insurance Operations department category.

## Overview

Core insurance functions including underwriting, claims, actuarial, and policy administration

## Departments

| Department | Code | Description |
|-----------|------|-------------|
| Claims | CLM | Claims intake, investigation, adjustment, settlement, and litigation management |
| Actuarial | ACT | Risk modeling, pricing, reserving, and actuarial analysis for insurance products |
| Policy Administration | POLAD | Policy issuance, endorsements, renewals, cancellations, and lifecycle management |
| Reinsurance | REINS | Reinsurance treaty negotiation, ceded premium management, and recovery processing |
| Special Investigations Unit | SIU | Insurance fraud detection, investigation, and referral for suspected fraudulent claims |
